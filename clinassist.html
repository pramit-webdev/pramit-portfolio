ClinAssist
Clinical AI Engine

ClinAssist is an AI-powered clinical documentation and coding system that converts doctor notes into ICD-10-coded, FHIR-compatible medical records.

End-to-end pipeline

Doctor note
→ LLM fact extraction
→ ICD-10 semantic engine
→ SOAP + insurance text
→ Database
→ FHIR R4 bundle

Why it matters

Hospitals, insurers, and ABDM require structured medical records. Doctors produce free-text. ClinAssist bridges this gap by converting raw clinical language into interoperable healthcare data.

What I built

  • • FAISS-powered ICD-10 semantic engine
  • • Longitudinal patient visit tracking
  • • FHIR R4 bundle generator
  • • Insurance-ready SOAP outputs
