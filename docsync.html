DocuSync

DocuSync is an AI-powered document intelligence platform that lets users search, summarize, and compare large collections of files using RAG.

Core capabilities

  • • FAISS semantic search
  • • LangGraph multi-agent workflows
  • • PDF, DOCX, CSV ingestion
  • • FastAPI backend

Why it’s valuable

Most organizations have thousands of unstructured documents. DocuSync turns them into a queryable knowledge system.
